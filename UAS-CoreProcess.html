01

Requirements Gathering

Define operational goals, technical criteria, integration needs, and management models before anything else is decided.

02

OEM & Aircraft Validation

Background checks on manufacturers, scrutiny of aircraft specs, and assessment of regulatory compliance — reliability, support, lifecycle cost, and interoperability tested against claims, not marketing.

03

Test-Flight Observation

Live flight operations run against Tactien-derived test cards, not vendor-preferred demonstrations. Blind testing and randomized missions expose what a controlled demo won't.

04

Data-Driven Analysis

Telemetry, operational data, and flight logs cross-referenced against the prescribed test conditions — not just what the vendor reports back.

05

Recommendation & Handoff

Findings compile into a clear, actionable report — but the phase ends when your team can operate what's been validated, without Tactien in the loop.
